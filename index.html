Gene Hunter

What is a Gene?

A gene is a sequence of DNA which encodes a gene product. They consist of building blocks of life, called nucleotides: A, C, G, and T.

Complementary Sequence

Your gene sequences run anti-parallel to one another. For every nucleotide base in your DNA, it is attached to an opposite pair. The opposite pairs read the opposite direction.

Motifs

Motifs are short recurring patterns in DNA which have biological functions. When DNA gets copied, there are specified sites machinery binds to.

- Let's see if there are any Motifs in your personal Gene!

1. "TATA" Boxes

2. "CGCG" Islands

3. "TAACGCG" Combination

If there are motifs present, you may be looking at a promoter which indicates the start area for DNA replication!
