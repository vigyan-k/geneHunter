- What is a Gene? -

A gene a sequence of coding in your DNA. Which encodes a gene product. They consist of building blocks called nucleotides of A, C, G, and T. These are the Sequence of life and make up who you are!

- Complementary Sequence -

Your gene sequences naturally run anti-parallel to one another. For every nucleotide base in your DNA, it is attached to an opposite pair. The opposite pairs flow the other way.

- Motifs -

Motifs are short recurring patterns in DNA which have biological functions. When DNA gets copied, there are specified sites machinery binds to.
